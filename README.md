The Legend of SOLDA

The Devourer of Liquidity

A retro-inspired action adventure built during the MONOLITH radiants DAO Solana Mobile Hackathon.

Inspired by classic 16-bit games like The Legend of Zelda, SOLDA introduces a blockchain-themed world where corrupted liquidity threatens the balance of multiple chains.

Players take control of Seeker, a wanderer tasked with restoring liquidity and defeating the Devourer of Liquidity.

---

Gameplay

- Explore a retro 16-bit world
- Fight corrupted creatures guarding broken liquidity pools
- Collect gems and artifacts
- Restore the balance of the chains (sacred Trees)

The game is designed with mobile gameplay in mind, featuring touch controls and fast action combat.

---

Story

Across the digital realm stand the Trees of Liquidity, each representing a blockchain ecosystem.

A dark force known as Binandorf – The Devourer of Chains has begun draining their liquidity, corrupting the guardians that once protected them.

Only the Seeker can restore the balance.

Defeat corrupted guardians, reclaim lost liquidity, and awaken the chains (trees) before everything collapses.

Vision

SOLDA aims to become a Zelda-like blockchain adventure where each chain is represented by a world, guardian, and liquidity mechanic.

Future systems may include:

- 10 different Sacred Trees (each representing important Blockchains)
- 10 Bosses with specificic battle/weapons/puzzle mechanics 
- On-chain assets
- NFT relics
- Cross-chain guardians
- Liquidity restoration mechanics
- Town with marketplace and stores
- Progression NFT that updates on your in game achievements (defeating bosses and progression)
